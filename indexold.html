Welcome to Del Brown's Network!

As a result of completing 4 courses through the University of Michigan, I have decided to create this Responsive Design Website. It is designed to look and perform well on any device, from phones to wide-screen monitors. CHeck it out on different devices to see what I mean.

On a phone or small tablet, the above navigation bar is replaced by the "burger" icon, which will produce a dropdown selection set. On wider display devices, a typical Navigation bar is visible.

Pressing the Home button will toggle this window to be shown or hidden. A Bootstrap class and accompanying Javascript function accomplish this. Likewise clicking on the "Video" link will show/hide the video section.
